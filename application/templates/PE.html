“I started at Land Registry as an Apprentice with very little IT knowledge and from a non-technical background. They helped me settle in and become familiar with different working areas and what they do, along with what they are currently working on. It also helped me to become familiar with IT and business terms which has helped guide me since joining a working team.
Since joining my team, digital Find Property Information I have been part of an agile environment, working alongside by buddy whom is a developer. He has given me a good insight into the work he does by breaking down difficult subject matters and explaining, sometimes through the use of drawings or tutorials.
This has also been broadened within my team by contributing in the daily scrum meetings and understanding their different roles and responsibilities. So far I have learnt the basics of various languages, starting with HTML and CSS and then moving onto learning about Python and the extension of Flask.
I am looking forward to contributing more with my team, this could be shadowing other roles such as a tester or user researcher and seeing how my team works with other areas so the service is successful.”
